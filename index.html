THE PLACE

The best rock bar in town

Hello, guys! Welcome to the Fans Rock Bar website! Rock music and heavy metal are in your blood, right? And you can't wait to listen to some outrageously awesome live music? Well, we have it all set up. Check out all our upcoming events, there surely is one that's right for you. Also, get a feel for the place and see how much fun we have each time by browsing through the gallery section of the website or by visiting our facebook page. Finally, come over and taste the high quality drinks we have on offer. See yah!
